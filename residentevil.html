STORY

Origins of the Outbreak

The nightmare began with the Umbrella Corporation, a powerful pharmaceutical company hiding dark secrets. Behind closed doors, Umbrella developed the T-Virus — a biological weapon capable of reanimating the dead and mutating living organisms into horrific creatures.

The T-Virus

The T-Virus attacks the human body at a cellular level, rewriting DNA and destroying the nervous system. Most infected become zombies — slow, aggressive, and driven by hunger. Others mutate into powerful Bio-Organic Weapons (B.O.W.s).

The G-Virus

The G-Virus is a powerful mutagen created by Dr. William Birkin that causes rapid, uncontrollable evolution. It transforms hosts into constantly mutating creatures with extreme regeneration and strength. Infected beings lose their humanity and become unstable bio-weapons driven by instinct and reproduction.

Raccoon City Incident

In 1998, the virus was accidentally released in Raccoon City. Within days, the entire population was infected. Survivors like Jill Valentine, Claire Redfield, Carlos Olivera and Leon S. Kennedy fought to escape as the city descended into chaos.

Umbrella's Downfall

After the Raccoon City disaster, the truth about Umbrella began to surface. The company was eventually exposed and taken down, but the damage was already done. The virus had spread, and new threats continued to emerge around the world.

Bio-Organic Weapons

From Tyrants to Hunters, Umbrella's experiments created deadly creatures designed for warfare. These monsters are stronger, faster, and far more dangerous than ordinary zombies.
